New way to manage your daily tasks!

Instructions

The index.html page contains the list of all tasks you have to do or you have done it. The finished tasks will be crossed (e.g the second task 'Go to cinema with friends' is already done). When you click on the arrow in front of the task and you will be redirected to the details of that task. The detils page show you the name of the task as well as the status finished or not and the description of the task. On the right side you find the picture and the name of the person. I made the navbar as well as the content responsive you can try it by changing the size of your browser window. That's all !!!
